Go Green Tips for Students

Created By

1)- Reduce,Reuse and Recycle

MINIMIZE WASTE: Avoid single use plastics like water bottles, disposable cups, and utensils.

RECYCYLE PROPERLY: Sort Trash into designated recycling bins.

REUSE ITEMS: Give old clothes, books, and furniture a second lifeby donating them to charity shops or selling them.

2)- Conserve Resources

SAVE WATER: Turn off the tap,shower when not in use.

SAVE ENERGY: Turn off lights,fans or any electrical equipment when not in use.

3)- Make Suistainable Choices

EAT LESS MEAT: Reducing meat consumption can significantly lower your carbon footprint.

SUPPORT LOCAL BUSSINESS MAN: Buying from local stores can reduce transportation emissons.

4)- Get Involved

JOIN ENVIRONMENTAL CLUBS: Connect with like-minded students and participate in campus suistanability initiatves.

ADVOCATE FOR CHANGES: Encourage your school or university to implement more suiistainable practices.

EDUCATE OTHERS: Share your knowledge and encourage friends and family to adopt eco-frindly hahits.
